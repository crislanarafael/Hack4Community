Save Your Environment by Starting an Exchange


Are you wanting to declutter your home? Do you find yourself unsure of what to do with unused clothing and textiles? Would you like to meet new people and contribute to environmental efforts in curbing pollution and waste?

We're introducing ReUp, an hub that facilitates clothing and textile exchanges in order to build stronger community ties and reduce environmental waste and pollution.

Users can connect with others to exchange their clothing and textiles with the intent that the receivers will, in good faith, reuse or upcycle the exchanged items. Users can thank the recipient for accepting the donated items with our digital Thank You token, a gift that can be shared on your social media accounts to show people what you're up what you're up to while also growing and engaging with the ReUp community.
