How to Save the Planet with ReUp in 3 Steps

1. Sign Up



Create your free ReUp account. All we need is your name, username, and an email and you're good to go!

To create and build our community, users need to be registered to access the Exchange Area, where you will begin your exchange with other useres, that is located on their dashboard.

Be kind and respectful when creating your public posts. And most of all, don't be afraid to make some new friends on your upcycling journey!

2. Arrange The Handoff



ReUp facilitates our user exchanges, though the rest is in your hands.

You and your exchanger are responsible for agreeing on what clothing and/or textiles you plan on exchanging as well as determining transportation and meeting logistics.

We encourage our users to connect with others based on location to further create those local community ties as well as our virtual ones, but we would love it if you decided to facilitate an international exchange.

It is up to you!

3. Reuse and Upcycle



By making our exchange area public on your dashboards, we are promoting a social contract between ReUp users. Completing an exchange means you intend to creatively repurpose what was given to you.

One feature we will implement in the near future is the ability for users to send digital "Thank You" tokens to each other. This can be shared on social media to show to your friends and families and reinforces our message that by participating with ReUp, you're doing a good job for yourself by gaining those skills in upcycling, and for your environment by preventing clothing and textiles to become landfill waste and reducing pollution created by producing more clothing and textiles than what's needed in our world!
